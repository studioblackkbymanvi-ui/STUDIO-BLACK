About Us

Where timeless Indian elegance meets modern sophistication.

At STUDIO BLACK by Manvi, we believe every outfit should tell a story. Based in Ludhiana, we specialize in handcrafted ethnic and modern couture designed with passion, precision, and purpose. Our collections blend traditional silhouettes with contemporary elegance — perfect for weddings, festivals, and statement moments.

From personalized fittings to custom embroidery and fabric curation, we’re committed to excellence at every stitch. With STUDIO BLACK, you're not just wearing a dress — you're wearing confidence.
